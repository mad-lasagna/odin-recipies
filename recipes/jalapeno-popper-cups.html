Jalapeño Popper Cups

Description

Jalapeño Popper Cups are a flavorful and bite-sized appetizer perfect for parties or snacking. They are made by filling small pastry cups, such as phyllo shells, wonton wrappers, or biscuit dough, with a creamy mixture of cream cheese, shredded cheddar, diced jalapeños, and sometimes crispy bacon bits for added texture and smoky flavor. The cups are baked until golden and bubbly, delivering a delightful combination of spicy, creamy, and savory in every bite. These handheld treats are easy to prepare, customizable, and sure to be a crowd-pleaser.

Ingredients

  • 12 mini phyllo tart shells
  • 4 ounces cream cheese, softened
  • ½ cup shredded Cheddar cheese
  • 2 jalapeno peppers, seeded and chopped
  • 1 tablespoon hot pepper sauce
  • bacon bits

Steps to make Jalapeño Popper Cups

  1. Preheat an oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Place phyllo cups onto a baking sheet.
  2. Stir together cream cheese, Cheddar cheese, jalapenos, and hot sauce in a bowl. Spoon mixture into phyllo cups. Sprinkle bacon bits on top. Bake in preheated oven until golden brown, about 15 to 20 minutes. Serve warm.
